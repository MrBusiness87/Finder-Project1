 ABOUT US:


Sports Finder is a simple app created to help people find and attend major sporting events near you. Simply enter your city or allow Sports Finder to know your location and it will send you results of events in your area. You can then buy your tickets and reserve your seats. After that, it's as easy as just showing up and enjoying the game!
